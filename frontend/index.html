How it works

From shop data to decision in three steps.

Designed for sellers applying for working capital and lenders evaluating a portfolio of digital merchants.

01

Capture seller inputs

Revenue, orders, returns, rating, shipping, inventory turnover, and operating history.

02

Score default risk

A Random Forest and Logistic Regression ensemble produces PD, risk tier, and signal flags.

03

Return a credit decision

The API returns approval status, credit limit, interest rate, reasoning, and portfolio records.

Live model preview

Switch the seller profile. Watch the decision change.

The model rewards operating consistency and penalizes return friction, late shipping, weak ratings, and short history.
