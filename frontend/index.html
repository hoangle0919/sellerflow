Why this exists

The best credit data isn't in a bureau file.

01

No history ≠ no signal

Most Vietnamese sellers have no CIC record. But 680 days of order data, customer ratings, and delivery performance is a richer behavioral dataset than any bureau provides.

02

Revenue you can verify

Platform revenue is timestamped, third-party verified, and updated daily. A payslip is a photocopy.

03

Risk priced correctly

Our model prices risk from operations — so strong sellers get 12.5% instead of the 40–80% informal market rate.

How it works

From shop data to decision.

1

Submit shop data

Operational metrics from your platform. Three minutes, no documents.

2

The model scores risk

A Random Forest and Logistic Regression ensemble produces a probability of default and signal flags.

3

Receive a decision

Approval status, credit limit, interest rate, and the reasoning behind each — in 1.2 seconds.

    Privacy
    
How we handle your data

    
What we collect

    
When you apply for an assessment, we store the business metrics you submit — shop name, platform, revenue, orders, returns, ratings, and operating history — together with the credit decision our model produces. When you join the access list, we store your email address.

    
How it is used

    
Submitted metrics are used solely to generate your credit assessment and to display it to authenticated lenders in the portfolio dashboard. Waitlist emails are used only to contact you about access. We do not sell data, and we do not share it with third parties beyond the hosting infrastructure the service runs on.

    
Retention and deletion

    
Data is retained while the service operates. To have your records deleted, write to lehuuhoang1909@gmail.com from the address you signed up with and we will remove them.

    
Status

    
SellerFlow is in pilot. The scoring model is a baseline trained on synthetic data and its output is not a lending commitment or an offer of credit.
