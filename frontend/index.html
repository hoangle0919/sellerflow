How it works

From shop data to a credit decision in three steps.

01

Connect shop data

Sellers share operating metrics from Shopee, TikTok Shop, or Lazada — revenue, returns, ratings, shipping speed. No bank statements.

02

The model scores risk

A Random Forest + Logistic Regression ensemble estimates probability of default across ten behavioral and financial signals.

03

Decide & disburse

Credit limit and rate are set as a function of monthly revenue and risk tier. Lenders approve and fund through existing rails.
